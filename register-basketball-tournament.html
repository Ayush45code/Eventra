About This Tournament

Get ready for the most exciting inter-department basketball championship of the year! This tournament brings together teams from all departments to compete for the coveted championship trophy and bragging rights.


Tournament Format:

  • Round-robin group stage followed by knockout rounds
  • Each department can field one team of 5 players + 2 substitutes
  • Professional referees and scorekeeping
  • Trophies and medals for winning teams
  • Sportsmanship awards for outstanding conduct

Requirements:

  • Must be a current student of the college
  • Basic basketball skills and understanding of rules
  • Commitment to attend all scheduled matches
  • Team spirit and sportsmanship
